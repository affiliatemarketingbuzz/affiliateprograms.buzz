Key Product Features

  • 24/7 automation — the AI employees work around the clock, handling tasks that would otherwise require human attention during business hours.
  • Single platform — instead of juggling subscriptions to separate content, SEO, and support tools, users access everything from one dashboard.
  • Intuitive design — the platform is built for non-technical users, making adoption fast even for small business owners who aren't tech-savvy.

The 70% Discount That Makes Selling Easy

Campaign Details: Commission, Cookie Window, and Payout

Understanding the commercial terms of the Sintra AI affiliate program helps you plan your promotional strategy and estimate potential earnings.

Commission Structure

  • Payout: 21% of the order sale amount — this is a strong percentage for a SaaS product. Unlike flat-rate commissions where you earn the same regardless of the plan purchased, percentage-based commissions mean larger purchases generate larger payouts.
  • Multiple conversions allowed — if the same referred user makes additional purchases or upgrades, you earn 21% on each transaction. This creates earning potential beyond the initial conversion.

Cookie Duration and Validation

  • Cookie duration: 30 days — when someone clicks your affiliate link, you receive credit for any purchase they make within the next 30 days. This is standard for SaaS products and provides adequate time for users to evaluate the platform before buying.
  • Validation period: 52 days — commissions are verified before payout to confirm that sales are legitimate and haven't been refunded. This protects both the advertiser and serious affiliates who drive quality traffic.

Tracking and Platform Support

  • Real-time tracking — monitor clicks, conversions, and pending commissions as they happen through your affiliate dashboard.
  • Web and mobile web support — conversions from both desktop and mobile visitors are tracked and credited.
  • Deeplink support — create affiliate links pointing to specific Sintra AI pages, features, or promotional offers rather than just the homepage. Sending users directly to the pricing page or a specific AI employee page improves conversion rates.
  • Worldwide geo — the campaign accepts traffic from any country, making it suitable for affiliates with international audiences.

How to Maximize Conversions With the Sintra AI Offer

Having access to a strong affiliate offer isn't enough — how you promote it determines your earnings.

Lead With the Discount

The 70% discount is your strongest conversion lever. Lead every piece of promotional content with this offer. Whether it's a blog post headline, video title, social media caption, or email subject line, the discount grabs attention and creates urgency.

"Sintra AI is offering 70% off right now" is a more compelling opening than "Sintra AI is an AI automation platform."

Build Trust Through Real Demonstrations

AI tool buyers want proof before purchasing. Content that shows Sintra AI actually working — screenshots of AI-generated content, before-and-after workflow comparisons, or screen recordings of the dashboard — converts significantly better than content that simply describes features.

Write short Sintra AI reviews that include specific examples of tasks the platform handles. Show how a content creator uses the AI content employee to draft blog outlines, or how a business owner uses the customer support employee to handle inquiries automatically.

Guide Users to Take Action

After building interest and trust, make the conversion step frictionless. Use direct links to the Sintra AI pricing or signup page (via deeplinks), highlight the active discount, and include clear calls to action. Coupon codes and cashback offers add additional incentive for price-sensitive buyers.

Use Multiple Touchpoints

Most buyers don't convert on first exposure. Use multiple channels to reach the same audience repeatedly — a blog post introduces the tool, a social media post reminds them of the discount, an email follows up with a comparison, and a video demonstrates the product. Each touchpoint moves the user closer to conversion.
