An affiliate program allows individuals to earn commissions by promoting products or services. You earn money when someone makes a purchase or completes an action through your referral link.

High-paying affiliate programs are usually found in SaaS, finance, and web hosting niches. Some programs offer recurring commissions or payouts up to 50% or more.

Beginners can start by choosing a niche, joining affiliate programs, and promoting products through blogs, social media, or YouTube. Consistent content and traffic are key to success.

Yes, most affiliate programs are completely free to join. You just need to sign up, get approved, and start promoting using your unique affiliate links.

Look for programs with good commission rates, reliable payouts, strong reputation, and products relevant to your audience. Cookie duration and support also matter.

Earnings vary widely depending on your traffic, niche, and strategy. Some beginners earn a few dollars, while experienced affiliates can generate thousands per month.

Cookie duration is the time period in which you can earn a commission after someone clicks your affiliate link. Longer durations increase your chances of earning commissions.
